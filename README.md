# Task 1– Sales Performance Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes online retail transaction data to evaluate sales performance, identify revenue trends, determine top-performing products and regions, and provide strategic recommendations for business growth.

The objective of this analysis was to answer key business questions regarding revenue generation, product performance, customer purchasing behavior, and expansion opportunities.

The dataset contains transactional sales records including product information, quantities purchased, unit prices, invoice dates, and customer locations.
Due to dataset size constraints, the repository contains the cleaned analytical dashboard and reporting workbook. The original dataset is publicly available from Kaggle as online retail sheet.
---

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Data Cleaning & Transformation
* Pivot Tables
* Pivot Charts

---

## Data Cleaning

The following data preparation steps were performed:

* Checked for duplicate records.
* Removed 10,624 records containing negative quantities.
* Removed 2,517 records with UnitPrice values less than or equal to zero.
* Verified date formatting for time-series analysis.
* Created a Revenue column using Quantity × UnitPrice.
* Created a Month-Year column to support trend analysis.
* Structured the dataset for Pivot Table and dashboard reporting.

These steps ensured that the analysis was based on valid sales transactions and reliable revenue calculations.

## Revenue Trends Over Time

### Objective

Understand how sales performance changes over time.

### Findings

Monthly revenue analysis revealed noticeable fluctuations in sales throughout the reporting period. Revenue increased significantly during certain months, indicating periods of stronger customer demand and purchasing activity.

### Insight

Sales performance is influenced by seasonal buying patterns and customer demand fluctuations. Peak revenue periods contribute disproportionately to overall business performance.

### Recommendation

Increase inventory availability, marketing efforts, and operational readiness during historically high-performing periods to maximize revenue opportunities.

---

## Product Performance Analysis

### Objective

Identify which products generate the most revenue.

### Findings

The analysis revealed that **DotCom Postage** generated the highest revenue among all products in the dataset.

### Insight

A small number of products contribute significantly to total revenue, demonstrating the importance of prioritizing high-performing products within inventory and marketing strategies.

### Recommendation

Maintain sufficient stock levels for top-performing products and increase promotional efforts around products with proven revenue-generating potential.

---

## Regional Performance Analysis

### Objective

Determine which regions generate the highest revenue.

### Findings

The **United Kingdom** generated the highest revenue and represents the company's strongest market.

### Insight

Revenue is concentrated within a small number of countries, with the United Kingdom serving as the primary driver of business performance.

### Recommendation

Continue investing in high-performing markets while exploring opportunities to expand sales and marketing efforts in emerging international markets.

---

## Business Growth Opportunities

The analysis identified several opportunities for sustainable business growth:

### Product Optimization

Focus inventory planning and promotional campaigns on products that consistently generate strong revenue performance.

### Market Expansion

Leverage success in high-performing regions while identifying growth opportunities in underrepresented markets.

### Seasonal Planning

Use historical sales trends to improve forecasting, inventory management, and marketing campaign timing.

### Customer Retention

Develop strategies that encourage repeat purchases and strengthen long-term customer relationships.

---

## Key Insights

### Insight 1

Revenue trends show clear fluctuations over time, suggesting the presence of seasonal demand patterns.

### Insight 2

DotCom Postage emerged as the highest revenue generating product, highlighting the importance of product level performance monitoring.

### Insight 3

The United Kingdom is the strongest-performing market and contributes a substantial share of total revenue.

### Insight 4

Removing invalid transactions and pricing records improved overall data quality and analytical reliability.

---

## Business Recommendations

### 1. Prioritize High Revenue Products

Increase inventory allocation and marketing support for products that consistently generate strong revenue.

### 2. Optimize Seasonal Operations

Align inventory planning and marketing campaigns with periods of historically high sales activity.

### 3. Strengthen High Performing Markets

Continue investing in regions that generate the highest revenue while maintaining customer satisfaction and retention.

### 4. Explore New Growth Markets

Identify countries with growth potential and develop targeted expansion strategies.

### 5. Improve Revenue Forecasting

Utilize historical sales trends to improve demand forecasting and operational planning.

---

## Conclusion

The analysis provided valuable insights into revenue performance, product profitability, and regional sales trends. The business generated total revenue of **R10,666,684.54** across **19,962 orders**, with **DotCom Postage** emerging as the highest revenue-generating product and the **United Kingdom** serving as the strongest-performing market.

By focusing on high-performing products, strengthening key markets, and leveraging seasonal sales patterns, the business can improve revenue performance and support sustainable long term growth.
